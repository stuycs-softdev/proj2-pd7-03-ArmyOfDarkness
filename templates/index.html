School Search: Learn About Schools in the United States

Mission Statement: Our goal is to provide interested parents and students with information regarding elementary, middle, and high schools as well as colleges and universities throughout the United States. We believe that parents and students deserve to make an informed decision. Thus, we will provide you with information through various search functions. You will be permitted to specify certain parameters and we will provide you with a list of all schools that match what you are looking for. More importantly, we will provide you with information about each of these schools.

About Us: We are the "Army of Darkness" - Jack Cahn, Tak Chi Wan, Alvin Leung and Haoxin Luo.

We are students at Stuyvesant High School in New York City taking Software Development. Many of us are ourselves in the process of our own "college searches" in which we are looking for college that are comptabile with our interests. That's why its important to us to provide you with information.
